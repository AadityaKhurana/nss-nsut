About NSS NSUT

🎯

Our Mission

To develop social consciousness and provide hands-on experience in community service among students of NSUT, fostering leadership and civic responsibility.

🌟

Our Vision

Creating socially aware and responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to society through sustainable community development initiatives.

💪

Our Impact

Over 500+ active volunteers making a difference in local communities through education, health awareness, and environmental conservation programs.

Our Activities

📚

Education Support

Teaching underprivileged children and organizing literacy programs in local communities.

🌱

Environmental Conservation

Tree plantation drives, waste management initiatives, and sustainability awareness campaigns.

🏥

Health & Wellness

Health camps, blood donation drives, and awareness programs on hygiene and nutrition.

🤝

Community Outreach

Rural development projects, disaster relief efforts, and social awareness campaigns.
